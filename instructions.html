Prompt Steps

  1. Open your desired AI chatbot. FOr these instructions we will be using ChatGPT
  2. Ask it to make a schedule for the month
  3. Write out your deadlines or have important events

    This could be birthday events to test days

  4. Plan out what task you may need for these events and how much time you want to allocate to these events

    For example if you have a test in two weeks you may need to study a total of 20 hours or if you have a birthday you may need to buy a present and that may take 2 hours

  5. Ask ChatGPT to plan a daily schedule based on those requirements and make adjustments as you go

    This includes sleeping schedule, work time preference, etc

  6. Get Creative

    Let's say you like to do your work at night but can't think of a good schedule. Ask ChatGPT to make the schedule more night owl based.

  7. Repeat for the next week

    Coming up with a schedule can be difficult and may take some time. Based on your previous week and what you could ask ChatGPT make adjustments as necessary

Now that you made your schedule, put it in a calendar.
